Everett Public Libraries

What can I do at the library?

The library is a quiet place where adults and children can come to read. Children and adults of all ages can select from a large collection of books of all genres and then barrow a few for two weeks to read at home. All you need is a free library card!

To obtain a free library card, you must bring:
  • An ID card (work ID, school ID, driver's license, Mass ID, etc.)
  • A piece of mail with your address
  • A school planner (for children)

Children of any age may obtain a library card.
