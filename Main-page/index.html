Who Are We?



El Salvador is one of the most amazing places because it has beautiful beaches and culture. It also has really good typical food, such as Pupusas. Pupusas are one of the most delicious plates that you can ever try. El Salvador also has many different states and every city has its own unique personality. In addition, El Salvador is hot almost all year. Therefore, it is a beautiful places to travel in the summer. You can visit mountains, beaches, and you can feel comfortable with some people you can meet.

Honduras is my country, a country full of many virtues and wonderful, beautiful things. You can find many places of tourism that are very precious. It is a country where people are very kind and humble. Of course, there are a few people who will not always be humble, but most are. Honduras is a country where the culture is well preserved. The food of the coast is very delicious, and the country also has some beautiful beaches where you can enjoy nice family time. Honduras is a tourist destination that attracts visitors for its abundant and varied, natural beauty that is manifested in its people, on its white and dark sand beaches, coral reefs, spectacular flora and fauna, and its archaeological history. Besides the country's natural beauty, its culture is expressed in the customs of its inhabitants as well as in its delicious typical food of the country, such as the 'baleada'.

Bangladesh is a very beautiful country. The name "Bangladesh" is a combination of the two Bengali words, "Bangla" and "Desh," meaning the country or land where the Bangla language is spoken. Bangladesh is one of the poorest and most densely populated countries in the world. The Bengali language is also the sweetest language in the world.

In Colombia, we love to eat empanas, and also we are really, really party people.

Vietnam is a Southeast Asian country known for its beaches, rivers, and bustling cities. The population is 95.54 million (2017). It's a beautiful country with the amazing Vietnamese language.
