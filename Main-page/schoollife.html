School Life

Your first year at Everett High School is going to be hard if you do not speak English because you will need to talk with people to guide you at lunch time or about your schedule. But, do not worry too much because all the teachers are trained to help people who do not speak English. So, if you don't know how school works, a teacher will help you guys.
