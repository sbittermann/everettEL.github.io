Zero Robotics

Interested in science, technology, engineering, or mathematics? Zero Robotics is a free summer program sponsored by MIT where Everett students compete as a team against other schools across the state, and even across the country! For this year's challenge, students must build a computer program that tells their robot how to navigate through trash in outer space and rescue the other damaged robot. The team with the most efficient code will have their code used by real astronauts on the International Space Station!

Every summer is a new challenge, so we are always looking for both new and returning team members!
